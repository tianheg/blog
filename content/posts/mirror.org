* 软件镜像设置
** pip
#+BEGIN_SRC sh
pip install pkg -i https://pypi.tuna.tsinghua.edu.cn/simple

# 修改 ~/.pip/pip.conf
[global]
index-url = https://mirrors.aliyun.com/pypi/simple/

[install]
trusted-host=mirrors.aliyun.com
#+END_SRC
