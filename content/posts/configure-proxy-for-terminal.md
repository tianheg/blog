## Zshrc

```zshrc
export http_proxy=http://127.0.0.1:1080
export https_proxy=http://127.0.0.1:1080

export http_proxy_user=user
export http_proxy_pass=pass

export https_proxy_user=user
export https_proxy_pass=pass
```
