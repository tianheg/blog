* 写作的重要之事

1. 写的行为很重要，写给谁看不重要。先能专注，之后做到极致专注。
2. 从这里 https://vixiss.bearblog.dev/writing-is-hard/ 得到的观点：
   - 一篇好文章要易于理解且用词准确
   - 对一篇文章进行不止一次修改：想到更好的用法就替换掉现在的用法，用AI检查可能的错误，确保想法通过语言准确地表达出来

#+BEGIN_QUOTE
Writing is hard work. A clear sentence is no accident. Very few sentences come out right the first time, or even the third time. Remember this in moments of despair. If you find that writing is hard, it's because it is hard. --On Writing Well: The Classic Guide to Writing Nonfiction, by William Zinsser
#+END_QUOTE
