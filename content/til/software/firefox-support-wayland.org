* firefox-support-wayland
:PROPERTIES:
:CUSTOM_ID: firefox-support-wayland
:END:
为 Firefox 添加环境变量：`MOZ_{ENABLEWAYLAND}=1`。

`~/.config/environment.d/envvars.conf`：

```conf MOZ_{ENABLEWAYLAND}=1 ```
