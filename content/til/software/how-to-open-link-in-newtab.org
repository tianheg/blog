In Firefox I use [[https://github.com/Infocatcher/Right_Links_WE][Right_Links_WE]] to archive that.

In Chrome, there are some ways to do that:

1. Ctrl + left click
2. Press mouse scroll wheel
3. Right-Click Menu -> open link in new tab
