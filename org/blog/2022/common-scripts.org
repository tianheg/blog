* 批量删除 Pypi 包

#+BEGIN_SRC sh

* 批量删除 Pacman 包

#+BEGIN_SRC sh
sudo pacman -Rs $(pacman -Qq | grep some_words)
# https://bbs.archlinux.org/viewtopic.php?pid=1533162#p1533162
#+END_SRC
